Marvel Superheroes

Peter Quill

Guardians of the Galaxy, Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2, Avengers: Infinity War, Avengers: Endgame

Peter Quill (also known as Star-Lord) is one of the main members of the Guardians of the Galaxy. He was captured by Ravagers after his mother passed away from cancer. He soon became a thief, which led him to meet the other members of the Guardians of the Galaxy. Later, he had to kill his evil father.

    Character Traits

  • Sort of Smart
  • Daring
  • Emotional

Tony Stark

Iron Man, Iron Man 2, The Avengers, Iron Man 3, Avengers: Age of Ultron, Captain America: Civil War, Spiderman: Homecoming, Avengers: Infinity War, Avengers: Endgame

Tony Stark (also known as Iron Man) was the owner of Stark Industries. He was a member of the Avengers, along with Bruce Banner, Steve Rogers, and many others. His parents were killed in a car accident when he was younger.

    Character Traits

  • Really smart
  • Sarcastic
  • Funny

Bill Lumbergh

Office Space

Bill Lumbergh is a manager at Initech. He is not liked by most of his co-workers because he always makes them work on Saturdays. Most of his sentences start with "Um, yeah", so he is known to be boring.

    Character Traits

  1. Boring

Forrest Gump

Forrest Gump

Forrest Gump is the owner of Bubba Gump Shrimp Company. He went to college on a football scholarship. After graduating, he joined the army, where he met Bubba and served in Vietnam. He likes to run, play ping pong, and be with Jenny.

    Character Traits

  1. Dumb
  2. Adventurous
  3. Polite

Chuck Noland

Cast Away

Chuck Noland is a FedEx engineer who became stranded on an island after a plane crash. He survived on that island for about 4 years, having to spear fish and search for fresh water. His best friend is Wilson the Volleyball.

    Character Traits

  1. Smart
  2. Innovative
